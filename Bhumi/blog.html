PUC IT FEST

Posted on February 1, 2024 by Abc

The "IT Fest" is an annual event dedicated to celebrating innovation, technology, and the digital world. It serves as a platform for students, professionals, and technology enthusiasts to come together and showcase their expertise, creativity, and advancements in the field of information technology.

During the IT Fest, various activities, competitions, workshops, and seminars are organized to explore different facets of IT. These may include coding competitions, hackathons, robotics challenges, cybersecurity workshops, and presentations on emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, and internet of things (IoT).

This year, PUC organized an IT fest featuring various contests for student participation. The event provided valuable opportunities for students to engage, learn, and showcase their talents, ultimately contributing to their personal and professional development.
